Volleyball is a team sport played between two teams of six players each. The objective of the game is to land the ball in the opponent's court in a way that the opposing team cannot return it properly. Here is a general explanation of volleyball :

    1. The Court :

  • The volleyball court is rectangular and divided into two halves by a net.
  • half measures 9 meters by 9 meters.

    2. Teams :

  • Each team consists of six players.
  • Teams are positioned on either side of the net, with players alternating between facing the net and facing away from it..

    3. The Net :

  • The net is 2.43 meters high for men and 2.24 meters for women.
  • It divides the court into two equal parts.

    4. The ball :

  • The volleyball is made of leather or synthetic material.
  • It weighs between 260 and 280 grams and has a diameter between 65 and 67 centimeters.

    5. The Serve :

  • The game begins with a serve, where a player hits the ball from the back line of their own court over the net into the opponent's court.
  • The server must adhere to rotation rules and must not touch the baseline during the serve.

    6. Gameplay :

  • The receiving team must return the ball to the opponent's court without letting it touch the ground.
  • Players can touch the ball up to three times to send it back, using passes, attacks, and blocks.
  • The objective is to score points by landing the ball in the opponent's court while preventing the opposing team from doing the same.

    7. Scoring :

  • A point is scored each time the ball touches the ground in the opponent's court.
  • Teams also earn points if the opponent commits a fault (e.g., the ball lands outside the boundaries, a player makes a double contact during a pass, etc.).

    • 8. Sets and Match::

  • A match is played in three winning sets (or five in some cases).
  • The first team to reach 25 points (or 15 in the fifth set, if necessary) with a two-point lead wins a set.
  • The team that wins two sets (or three in some cases) wins the match.

Volleyball is a fast-paced and dynamic sport, emphasizing teamwork, strategy, and the precision of passes and attacks.
